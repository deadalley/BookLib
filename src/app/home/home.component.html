Book Lib

Your personal library. Everywhere you go.

  • Manually add books to your library with title, author, title in original language, publisher, year of publication and number of pages
  • Customize your books: give them a rating, separate them in categories such as favorite, owned, read or in wishlist and add personalized notes to them, like a review or thoughts you had while reading
  • Categorize and organize your books by adding tags and genres to them or adding them to a collection
  • BookLib allows you to separate your books in collections. Collections can have as many books as you want and can be organized any way you want
  • Have a favorite trilogy? Want to put together all those hundreds of books written by your favorite author? Create a new collection and organize your books according to your needs
  • Share updates about books and collections in your library with friends over Facebook or Twitter
  • Invite friends to create their own personal libraries and see what books they are reading or want to read next
  • Import book data from millions of books available in the Goodreads and Amazon databases
  • Connect your BookLib account to your Amazon or Goodreads account and import your book favorites and wishlist easily
  • Keep track of your favorite authors and their work by accessing the Goodreads database directly from BookLib
  • Tired of keeping tracking of your books in boring spreadsheet files? Import those files directly into BookLib and start managing your personal library in a much more intuitive way!
  • Access your personal library anywhere and show the world your impressive collection!
