Welcome to your new digital library!

BookLib lets you organize your books in a smart way. Take a tour around the application and see what amazing features you can discover ;).
Books

Here you find ALL of your books. You can see them in tiles or in a list, organize them by name, author, language or year. You can also filter them, search them and add new books manually. This is where your library starts!

Collections

You can organize your books in collections, like series of books, trilogies or books that have something in common. Find all of your collections here, add new ones and manage your current ones.

Authors

Here you can find info about authors you follow. (This feature is still under development!)

Goodreads

Here you can connect to the Goodreads platform, import your library from your account, find books and authors and connect your current books to the Goodreads database. (This feature is still under development!)

Import

Here you can import your personal library from a customized spreadsheet and have all your books very easily uploaded to your digital library.
